About

I am an HTML Email Developer, Website, and Graphic Designer who has worked with individuals and entrepreneurs, who had an idea that they would like to see turn into digital art. On every project, I work with clients to help them achieve the best results possible for their campaign. My wide range of projects includes developing designs for athletic fan campaigns, start-up apparel businesses, family and friends in a freelance capacity. I have been trained and completed a graphic communication program, where I learned to utilize tools, such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I was also awarded a certificate in HTML, CSS, and Email Design. I hold a Certificate of Achievement in Screen Printing from Palomar College in San Marcos, CA. My experience and time in this field have benefited me when it comes to designing for web or print. Whether if its creating, re-creating, or combining, I take each project as a opportunity in turning my designs and their ideas into art we both can benefit from it.

Please feel free to look at my "PORTFOLIO" and you are more than welcome to "CONTACT".

Thanks for visiting
